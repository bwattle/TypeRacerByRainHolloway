HOW TO PLAY

TypeRacer is a very simple, easy to play game.

When you press the start button the game begins and the timer starts at 60 seconds. A word appears in the middle of the screen and you are required to type this word as fast as possible in the provided text box. Once you have finished typing the word, press spacebar to continue on to the proceeding word; as simple as normal typing. If you typed the word correctly, the word will be highlighted green, however if you were incorrect, the word will be highlighted red.

When you are typing, letters that are correct will be highlighted green and letters that are typed incorrectly will be highlighted red. This allows you to identify mistakes quickly and easily.

You do not have to type the entire word before moving onto the next word. If you find the word too difficult to type or you have made too many mistakes to backtrack, simply press spacebar and keep moving on. A score is kept track, incrementing each time a word is entered correctly.

The timer will turn red when you are approaching the end of the game at 10 seconds. When the timer hits 0, the game will end and you will be prompted for an anonymous username. If you beat one of the current 10 high-scores (Max of 10 high-scores), the previous high-score will be replaced with your username and score.

You can click the start button at any time to restart the game.
